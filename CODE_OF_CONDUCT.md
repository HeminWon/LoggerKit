# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

To foster an open, respectful, and collaborative community, maintainers and contributors pledge to:

- Respect people with different backgrounds and viewpoints
- Accept constructive feedback
- Focus discussions on facts and technical issues
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Insults, threats, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Public or private personal attacks
- Any form of malicious stalking or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and private communication channels.

## Enforcement

If you observe unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer at `heminwmh@gmail.com`.
Maintainers may remove content, lock discussions, reject contributions, or take further action when necessary.
